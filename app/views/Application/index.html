Welcome to In2EverNote

This is a sample web mashup utility written in scala with the Play! Framework running on Google App Engine.

First login to LinkedIn (using oauth) Then enter in your evernote username and password -- we do not store your evernote username or password. You will then be able to save important shares from people in your LinkedIn network to EverNote.
